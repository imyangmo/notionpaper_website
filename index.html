What is

Notion Paper

Notion Paper is a tool that helps you use Notion like a CMS, you can edit your site on Notion, and it will generates a static site for you.

Scenarios

Use Notion to blog

Now you can!
Focus on writing, Notion is a great What-You-See-is-What-You-Get writing tool, Notion Paper will do the rest for you.

Use Notion to wiki

You can... in the future...
Notion Paper is planning to build more themes for each scenario, stay connected!

Usage

Notion Paper NOW
BETA

Use Notion Paper Now, the online service, to generate your site in four easy steps!

Run Locally
BETA

You could also download the source code, and run it on your computer.
